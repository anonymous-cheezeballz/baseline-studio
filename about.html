About Baseline Studio

Baseline Studio was born out of a simple observation: many great small businesses don’t have an online presence that reflects the care and quality behind their work. Too often, their website is either missing entirely or fails to capture what makes the business feel distinct.

I started Baseline Studio to close that gap. Design isn’t just about how something looks—it’s about how it performs, how it communicates, and how it represents the brand and the people behind it. That perspective carries into every project, shaping websites that are clean, intentional, and visually compelling.

Baseline Studio designs and builds high-quality websites that balance aesthetics with function.
