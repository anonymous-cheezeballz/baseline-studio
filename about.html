About Baseline Studio

Baseline Studio was born out of a simple observation. Many of the best small businesses we encountered had little to no online presence, or one that failed to reflect the care and quality of their work. If you didn’t happen to drive by or hear about them through word of mouth, you’d never know they existed—resulting in missed opportunities and overlooked experiences.

We decided to do something about it. With backgrounds in graphic design and finance, we set out to bridge that gap. Our work goes beyond building websites—we help business owners give shape to their vision through design that’s thoughtful, functional, and visually compelling.

Baseline Studio designs and builds high-quality websites that balance aesthetics with function.
